About TN Elections 2026

The Tamil Nadu Elections 2026 will determine the next government of the state. Various political parties will participate in the elections and campaign across Tamil Nadu to gain public support.

Political Parties Participating

  • DMK - Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam
  • AIADMK - All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam
  • BJP - Bharatiya Janata Party
  • INC - Indian National Congress
  • NTK - Naam Tamilar Katchi
  • PMK - Pattali Makkal Katchi
